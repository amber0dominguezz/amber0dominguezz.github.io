About Amber.

Amber Dominguez Penaloza is a Digital Media Art undergraduate using technology as her instrument of purpose. Throughout her time exploring different mediums, she found that code was the thing that made her heart fly. While she is currently learning more coding languages in her spare time, she is also perfecting her understanding of the basics.
