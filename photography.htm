Video Piece

This is a video piece I made using Adobe Premiere Pro and an old Canon point & shoot camera. It was created with the thought of having two people within one. As someone who struggles personally with Dissociation and Drealization disorders, I often find myself not in tune with myself and who I am. This video piece shows my interpretation of my two sides. The presentable, casual, and normal girl with her inner counterpart, the dreamlike, intriguing, and seductive girl.
