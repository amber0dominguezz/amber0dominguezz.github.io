3D Print

In this project, we created sculptures in the video game Minecraft and got the chance to 3D print them and paint them. We used the applications MeshLab, FlashPrint 5, and Mineways. The smaller version of the Hylian Shield print on the right is the first test version, the bigger one on the left is the final with more 3D aspects and painting done.
